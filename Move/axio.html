Policy & Procedures

DRIVER RESTRICTIONS/UNDERAGE SURCHARGE

Drivers must be between the ages of 21 and 75 years of age to drive cars and between 25 and 75 years of age to drive vans, buses and luxury SUVs. Driver’s Licence must be at least 1 year old for persons between the ages of 23 – 75 years and 3 years for persons under 23 years. For buses, vans and luxury SUVs the persons Driver’s Licence must be a minimum of 3 years old.

CREDIT CARDS

We accept all credit cards.

SMOKE-FREE VEHICLES

Smoking is not permitted in our rental vehicles. Special cleaning charges may apply for excessive odours and burns. Please check with our counter agents for more details

CANCELLATIONS

There are no cancellation fees applicable for car rental services. However, a cancellation fee may apply for Private Transfers or other ground transportation services which have been booked and where we have not been notified in good time that the chauffeur is no longer needed.

HEARING-IMPAIRED CLIENTS

Persons that are hearing-impaired will not be allowed to drive our vehicles as our insurers currently do not provide coverage.

DELIVERY/COLLECTION OF VEHICLES

Free delivery and pickup within a one mile radius of any branch location
